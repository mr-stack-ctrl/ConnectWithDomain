The Spirit of 1919 Birmingham

Gangsters & Goons
The underworld runs deep in Birmingham. Organized crews with slicked-back hair, tailored suits, and Cuban cigars dominate smoky backrooms. Their revolvers are calling cards of power and authority in a lawless underworld.
The Law & Order
Police walk the beat with steel at their side. Their revolvers represent justice and the thin blue line separating chaos from civilization. Every shot fired is a decision with consequences echoing through the city.
Street Survivors
Common folk carry iron for protection. In factories and streets of Birmingham, a revolver means survival. It speaks louder than words and commands respect faster than argument in a city without mercy.
